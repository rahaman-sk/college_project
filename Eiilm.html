EIILM-Kolkata at 47th International Kolkata Book Fair

From January 18, 2024
To January 31, 2024

Eastern Institute for Integrated Learning in Management Kolkata or EIILM-Kolkata is an education institute in Kolkata, India. It was that founded in 1995 and is affiliated with Vidyasagar University, Raiganj University, and Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology.

It's that time of the year when bibliophiles across India rejoice! It's the 47th International Kolkata Book Fair. And EIILM-Kolkata is back at the prestigious venue at Stall Number 299, near Gate 6. Join us in celebrating reading from 18 January 2024 onwards.
